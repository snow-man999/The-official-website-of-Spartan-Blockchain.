SPARTAN BLOCKCHAIN
COMMUNITY MEETUP 2019

Friday, March 29th | Broad Art Lab - East Lansing, MI | 6:00PM - 9:00PM

On the evening of March 29th, Spartan Blockchain hosted a Greater Lansing Blockchain community meetup at the Broad Art Lab on Grand River Avenue. The Spartan Blockchain Community Meetup gave attendees the opportunity to network with invited blockchain & fintech firms from around the Metro Detroit area to discuss the blossoming educational community and startup ecosystem centered around blockchain and its applications around the Midwest. Free admission to the event was complemented by light food, drinks, and a live band.

SPARTAN BLOCKCHAIN INNOVATION COMPETITION 2019

Saturday, March 30th | MSU Innovation Center - East Lansing, MI | 8:30AM - 5:30PM

The Spartan Blockchain Innovation Competition brought together teams from leading academic institutions to compete in a thought-provoking blockchain pitch competition. The event schedule fostered innovation and collaboration through ample networking opportunities spread across technology spaces located around Michigan State's campus. Attendees gained access to professionals representing enterprise blockchain industry leaders and pitched solutions leveraging blockchain technology to address society's rapidly changing needs. We are proud to have provided an inclusive environment for students to interact with disruptive technologies and continue their development as leaders in the blockchain space.
