WHO ARE WE?

Spartan Blockchain Solutions (SBS) is a student-run, non-profit blockchain advisory and advocacy firm. Our mission is to provide local businesses and organizations with innovative blockchain solutions while jumpstarting Michigan State Universityâ€™s ecosystem to become a leading academic institution in the blockchain space. Our analysts develop skills through internal workshops, client engagements, and networking with professionals in the blockchain space.
