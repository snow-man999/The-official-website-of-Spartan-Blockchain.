CONSULTING SERVICES

Spartan Blockchain Solutions (SBS) is a student-run, nonprofit blockchain advisory firm which aims to provide local businesses and organizations with innovative blockchain solutions while developing our student consultants and developers as leaders in the blockchain space. The SBS Engagement Team interacts with clients to provide value and solve complex business problems by leveraging some of the best software platforms in the blockchain space.
