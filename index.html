WHO ARE WE?

We are a nonprofit blockchain advisory firm and student organization dedicated to making Michigan State University a global leader in blockchain development and innovation. We also strive to serve Michigan State University and the surrounding East Lansing area by helping students, faculty, and businesses adopt blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies into their everyday lives.

We firmly believe that blockchain technology will revolutionize the way we perceive value and information. Large-scale adoption of blockchain systems will completely restructure our global economy in ways that we haven't seen since the industrial revolution. We want to prepare our students and faculty so that they can obtain positions that would allow them to become industry leaders in the blockchain revolution.

OUR PILLARS

MOTIVATE

We motivate students to take an active role in the blockchain revolution.

EDUCATE

We teach students the complex nature of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies, without bias.

INNOVATE

We innovate to provide the world a better future.

COLLABORATE

We foster diversity, collaboration, and inclusion in our community and organization.
